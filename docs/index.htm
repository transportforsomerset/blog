First Buses of Somerset pull out of Taunton Park and Ride Contract.

First Buses of Somerset have informed Somerset Council that they intend to pull out of the local contract to operate the Taunton Park and Ride service, which runs buses back and forth between the Silk Mills and Gateway car parks.

The company claim operating the service is no longer viable for them from a business perspective.

First Buses of Somerset have operated the Taunton Park & Ride since 2014, when they became the interim contractor after WebberBus lost the contract due to complaints about the service. First Buses of Somerset later became the permanent operator of the service.

Somerset Council put the contract out to tender, and awarded the contract to Stagecoach South West, who it's believed will operate the service with buses based at their Tiverton depot.
