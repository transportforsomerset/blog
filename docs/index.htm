First Buses of Somerset could withdraw the Yeovil based 54 and 58 bus services in April

First Buses of Somerset have informed Somerset Council that they may have to withdraw the Yeovil based 54 and 58 services in April of this year.

Previously, Somerset Council announced that subsidies paid to First Bus to help support local services may be cut, and as a result, First Bus say they are unable to maintain the current services without that support.

As a result, the loss making 54 and 58 services may be withdrawn entirely, unless Somerset Council commits to maintaining the subsidy.

At the same time, First Buses of Somerset stated that the 25 and 28 Taunton based services would also have to be reduced, again due to loss of a subsidy.

Posted on 6th February 2024 by TfS.

Stagecoach confirm double-decked buses with a 20 minute frequency for Taunton Park and Ride service

Stagecoach, who will take over the operation of Taunton's Park and Ride service, have confirmed they will be using double-decker bues with a new livery, with a bus every 20 minutes in each direction. This means there will be 3 buses per hour from both the Silk Mills and Gateway car parks.

The service will run between 6am and 8pm, Monday to Friday, with a later start on Saturday's. The service will not operate on Sunday's.

First Buses of Somerset service 54 between Taunton and Yeovil at risk

The First Buses of Somerset service that links Taunton to Yeovil is at risk, due to Somerset Council funding due to end in March.

Route 54, which also serves Langport, Somerton, and Ilchester, currently operates every two hours, and has six trips a day in each direction.

First Buses of Somerset pull out of Taunton Park and Ride Contract.

First Buses of Somerset have informed Somerset Council that they intend to pull out of the local contract to operate the Taunton Park and Ride service, which runs buses back and forth between the Silk Mills and Gateway car parks.

The company claim operating the service is no longer viable for them from a business perspective.

First Buses of Somerset have operated the Taunton Park & Ride since 2014, when they became the interim contractor after WebberBus lost the contract due to complaints about the service. First Buses of Somerset later became the permanent operator of the service.

Somerset Council put the contract out to tender, and awarded the contract to Stagecoach South West, who it's believed will operate the service with buses based at their Tiverton depot.
